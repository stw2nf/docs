**The RC Controller allows you to manually control your PAL platform with precision and ease. This guide outlines the features and functionality of the RC Controller**


### Getting Started

1. Ensure the RC Controller is fully charged
2. Power on your PAL platform
3. Pair the controller with your platform
4. Begin manual control operations

### Controls

- Joystick controls
- Button functions
- Emergency stop
