## PAL Flatbed Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential to keep your PAL Flatbed operating at peak performance.

### Maintenance Schedule

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Daily Checks">
    - Visual inspection of platform
    - Check battery and fuel levels
    - Inspect wheels and tires
    - Verify all systems operational
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Weekly Maintenance">
    - Clean platform exterior
    - Check electrical and mechanical connections
    - Inspect hybrid system components
    - Review system logs
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monthly Maintenance">
    - Comprehensive system check
    - Battery and engine health assessment
    - Software updates
    - Calibration checks
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Annual Service">
    - Full system inspection
    - Component replacement as needed
    - Professional servicing
    - Certification renewal
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
